About Helena

  • Population: 33,885
  • Year of Incorporation: 1881
  • Region: West-Central Montana
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Household Income: $64,798, slightly lower than the state average ($67,631)

History

The Helena area has provided the setting for thousands of years of Native Americans, the Lewis & Clark Expedition, the wanderings of fur trappers & gold prospectors, homestead farmers, key transcontinental railroad lines, and the state capital. Helena is a community with a colorful history.
